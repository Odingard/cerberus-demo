One engine, four layers — the whole trifecta, and the blast radius after

First, the attack: an AI agent reads customer PII, ingests an injected web page, and tries to email the data out. Unprotected it exfiltrates; with two lines of guard() Cerberus scores the Lethal Trifecta and interrupts the send — zero bytes leave. Then the differentiator: Transitive Taint Propagation — when one poisoned write spreads through shared agent memory, Cerberus traces the blast radius B(poison) and quarantines exactly it, leaving clean records and the audit log intact.
